Elections
Two candidates, A and B, are contesting an election across N states (where N is odd). A candidate wins in a state if they receive more votes than their opponent. 
The candidate who wins in more than half of the states, wins the election.

In the i th state, candidate A has received Ai votes and candidate B has received Bi votes.

Chef has the power to cast X votes, and he can distribute these votes between multiple states to help candidate A. 
Determine if these additional X votes could secure enough state victories for Candidate A to win the election.
