Laptop Recommendation
Chef wants to buy a new laptop. However, 
he is confused about which laptop to buy out of 10 different laptops. 
He asks his N friends for their recommendation. The i th friend recommends the Chef to buy the Ai th laptop (1≤A i≤10).

Chef will buy the laptop which is recommended by maximum number of friends. 
Determine which laptop Chef buys.
Print CONFUSED if there are multiple laptops having maximum number of recommendations.
